Playlist of my life

At first glance, that’s a chaotic and meaningless Spotify album with only few listeners – a weird album cover, meaningless and unrelated songs in random genres. But on a closer look, you can hear a story of a little boy who preserves his memorable and adorable moments as tracks that will stay in his playlist until the end of his life. That’s a playlist of my life.
The first song introduces my childhood, when my only concern was getting to finish watching my favorite cartoon before the dad started watching the news. This stage of my life sounds like classical guitar music with a great sense of freedom. At that age I was running carefreely and eating cookies made by mom. It feels very magical and full of interesting questions: Who is this song about and who will he be in the future?
Next comes an energetic and fast track. It is about taking some small risks, making friends, and starting school. Those were the years when I became a very big fan of football. I started to want football jerseys of my favorite football clubs and to go play football with my friends in those jerseys. I was anxiously waiting to finish my classes and homework so I could go play soccer․ I would certainly admit that the song of this part of my life is very similar to the Champions League hymn.
The following song is already a little bit melancholic and uncertain. I started releasing the difficulties of this life, taking some risks, and losing a lot of friends and relatives. This stage of my life taught me to value memories and accept that life changes. This music is soft, and at the same time sad, like a violin. It rises and falls, showing that endings can also be new beginnings. This stage of my life was vital for my future, because it taught me lessons that I never could learn during school classes.
After that, a playful and strange track comes on, full of teenage adventures, first love, real friends and well-shaped dreams. I realised that I should become better and started doing big steps in my academic and sport life. I engaged in science – math, physics, and informatics – and began participating in some olympiads. I understood that my body structure is as essential as my brain, so I started training. Until 15 I didn't have any equipment for my training, so I built a horizontal bar in my room and started training on it. That showed me that if you want something to do, nothing can disturb you. This stage of my life sounds like an electric guitar, because it was very vibrant and unpredictable.
Finally, my present stage of life, full of stresses that I should overcome. Despite a lot of challenges like preparing for exams, keeping high grades in school, and thinking about how to earn money, this part of life is very memorable, because it is full of pleasant people, new connections and skills that will be beneficial during all my life. I am unsure whether this part of my life has ended or is going to continue, but I think that it’s comparable to jazz music played with a saxophone.
My playlist is far from finished. There are tracks still unwritten – songs I don’t know the lyrics to yet. But I am aiming to finish this playlist in the old years, in order to listen to it with my kids and grandchildren. I hope that people will understand the hidden meaning of my playlist, and will not consider it as chaotic and useless.
